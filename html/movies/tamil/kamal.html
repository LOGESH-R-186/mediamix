Kamala Haasan,is born on November 7, 1954, in Paramakudi, Tamil Nadu, Kamala Haasan began his film career as a child actor in the early 1960s.He is called as "Ulaga Nayagan" by his fans. He gained fame as an adult actor in the 1970s and 1980s, delivering iconic performances in films like Apoorva Raagangal (1975), Nayakan (1987), and Indian (1996). Kamala Haasan is a prominent Indian actor, filmmaker, and politician known for his work in Tamil cinema, as well as in Telugu, Malayalam, Hindi, and Kannada films. He is celebrated for his versatility and has acted in a wide range of genres, from action to drama, comedy, and even experimental cinema.
