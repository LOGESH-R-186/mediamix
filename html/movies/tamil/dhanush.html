Dhanush,is born Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja on July 28, 1983, is a prominent Indian actor, producer, director, playback singer, and lyricist primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. He has established himself as one of the most versatile and talented actors in the industry, known for his ability to effortlessly portray a wide range of characters.Dhanush made his acting debut in 2002 with the film "Thulluvadho Ilamai," directed by his father, Kasthuri Raja, and written by his brother, Selvaraghavan. The film, which dealt with the struggles of adolescence, was a sleeper hit, and Dhanush's performance was well received. Dhanush achieved nationwide recognition with the 2011 film "Aadukalam," directed by Vetrimaaran. His portrayal of a cockfighter in rural Tamil Nadu earned him the National Film Award for Best Actor, making him one of the youngest actors to receive this prestigious honor. The film also won several other National Awards and solidified Dhanush's reputation as a powerhouse performer.
