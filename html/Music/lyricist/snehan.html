Snehan, born as Sivaselvam on June 23, 1978, is a Tamil lyricist, poet, and actor known for his work in the Tamil film industry. He was born in Pudhukariyapatti, a small village in the Thanjavur district of Tamil Nadu. Snehan's journey to becoming a renowned lyricist is inspiring, as he came from humble beginnings and faced several challenges before making his mark in the industry. Snehan gained further popularity when he participated in the Tamil reality show Bigg Boss Tamil Season 1, hosted by actor Kamal Haasan, in 2017. He finished as the runner-up of the season, which brought him closer to the audience and showcased his personality beyond his professional work.
