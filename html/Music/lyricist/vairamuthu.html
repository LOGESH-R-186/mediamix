Vairamuthu,is a prominent Tamil poet, lyricist, and author from India, known for his significant contributions to Tamil literature and cinema. He was born on July 13, 1953, in Mettur, a village in Tamil Nadu. Vairamuthu's work spans several decades, and he has written lyrics for over 7,500 songs in Tamil cinema, collaborating with many of the industry's leading music directors, including Ilaiyaraaja and A.R. Rahman. Vairamuthu has won numerous awards for his work, including the National Film Award for Best Lyrics seven times and the prestigious Sahitya Akademi Award for his contribution to Tamil literature.
