Vaali,is born as T. S. Rangarajan on October 29, 1931, was a legendary Tamil poet and lyricist known for his immense contribution to Tamil cinema. Over a career spanning more than five decades, Vaali wrote lyrics for thousands of songs, making him one of the most prolific and celebrated lyricists in Tamil film history. He worked with several renowned music directors, including M.S. Viswanathan, Ilaiyaraaja, and A.R. Rahman.
