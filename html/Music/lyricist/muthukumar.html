Na.Muthukumar,born as Nagarajan Muthukumar on July 12, 1975, in Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu, was a celebrated Tamil lyricist, poet, and author. He was one of the most prolific and respected lyricists in Tamil cinema, known for his profound and emotive lyrics that resonated deeply with audiences. Muthukumar won the National Film Award for Best Lyrics twice: for the song "Anandha Yazhai" from the film Thanga Meengal in 2013 and for "Azhage Azhage" from Saivam in 2014. These accolades underscored his talent and contribution to Tamil cinema.
