G.V.Prakash Kumar,is Born on June 13, 1987, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, he is the nephew of the famous Indian music director A. R. Rahman. G. V. Prakash made his debut as a film composer at a young age and quickly established himself as one of the leading music directors in the industry. He first gained widespread recognition with the soundtrack for the Tamil film Veyyil (2006), which was well-received. However, his breakthrough came with the music for the critically acclaimed movie Polladhavan (2007). Since then, he has composed music for numerous successful films, including Aadukalam (2010), Darling (2015), Theri (2016), and Soorarai Pottru (2020).
