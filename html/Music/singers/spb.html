S.P.Balasubramaniam,is born on June 4, 1946, in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh, he was one of the most versatile and celebrated singers in the Indian music industry. He recorded over 40,000 songs in various languages, including Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hindi, and Malayalam, over a career spanning five decades. SPB's contributions to Indian music are immense, and he was known for his melodious voice, which could adapt to different genres and emotions. He was the recipient of numerous awards, including the National Film Award for Best Male Playback Singer six times and the Padma Shri and Padma Bhushan from the Government of India for his contributions to the arts.
