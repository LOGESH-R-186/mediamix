Vijay Antony ,is born on July 24, 1975, in Nagercoil, Tamil Nadu, India, started his career in the music industry before transitioning into acting. He initially worked as a sound engineer and then made his debut as a music composer with the film Sukran in 2005.Vijay Antony is an Indian music composer, playback singer, and actor primarily associated with Tamil cinema. He gained prominence in the film industry with his music compositions, particularly for songs like "Naaka Mukka," which became highly popular. In addition to his work as a music director, Vijay Antony has also acted in several films, often playing lead roles. Some of his notable films as an actor include Naan (2012), Salim (2014), and Pichaikkaran (2016).
