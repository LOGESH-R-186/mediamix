Shreya Ghoshal,is Born on March 12, 1984, in Berhampore, West Bengal, Shreya has made an indelible mark in the Indian music industry, earning her a place among the most iconic singers of her generation.Shreya Ghoshal's debut in Devdas was nothing short of sensational. She lent her voice to several songs in the film, including "Bairi Piya," "Dola Re Dola," and "Silsila Ye Chaahat Ka." Her performance in these songs was widely appreciated, and she won several awards, including the National Film Award for Best Female Playback Singer and the Filmfare Award for Best Female Playback Singe
