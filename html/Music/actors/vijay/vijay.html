Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar,is born on June 22, 1974, is one of the most popular and influential actors in the Tamil film industry, also known as Kollywood.He is widely referred to as "Thalapathy" by his fans. Vijay is known for his charismatic performances, mass appeal, and ability to connect with audiences across different age groups. His films often feature a mix of action, comedy, romance, and social messages, making him a versatile actor.Vijay's transformation into a "mass hero" began in the early 2000s with films like "Ghilli" (2004), a blockbuster that solidified his place as a top star in Tamil cinema. Directed by Dharani, Ghilli was a turning point in Vijay's career and remains one of his most iconic films.
