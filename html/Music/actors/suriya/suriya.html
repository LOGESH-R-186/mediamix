Suriya,is born Saravanan Sivakumar on July 23, 1975, is a highly acclaimed Indian actor, producer, and philanthropist known for his work predominantly in the Tamil film industry. He is one of the most versatile and respected actors in Indian cinema, often praised for his ability to portray a wide range of characters with depth and nuance..He is widely referred to as "Nadippin Nayagan" by his fans. Suriya made his acting debut in 1997 with the film "Nerrukku Ner," directed by Vasanth and produced by Mani Ratnam. Although the film was a moderate success, it marked the beginning of Suriya's acting career.Suriya's breakthrough came with the 2001 film "Nandha," directed by Bala. His portrayal of a troubled young man trying to reintegrate into society after serving time in a juvenile home was widely appreciated and earned him his first Tamil Nadu State Film Award for Best Actor.
