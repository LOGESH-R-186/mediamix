Hiphop Thamizha Aadhi,is born in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. He developed an interest in music at a young age, particularly in hip-hop and rap, which were relatively new genres in the Indian music scene at the time. Aadhi made his acting debut in the film Meesaya Murukku (2017), which he also wrote and directed. Following the success of Meesaya Murukku, Aadhi acted in and directed other films like Natpe Thunai (2019), Naan Sirithal (2020), and Anbarivu (2022). These films, often infused with his musical style, were appreciated by his fans.
