Ilaiyaraaja, Born on June 2, 1943, in Pannaipuram, Tamil Nadu, Ilaiyaraaja has had a prolific career spanning several decades and has composed music for over 1,000 films across various languages, including Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, and Hindi.He is referred to as "Isaignani" (The Musical Genius) by his fans. Some of his most iconic works include scores for films like "Thalapathi," "Nayakan," "Punnagai Mannan," and "Sindhu Bhairavi."Ilaiyaraaja has composed numerous devotional and classical albums. He has received many awards and honors, including the prestigious Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan, recognizing his immense contribution to Indian music.
