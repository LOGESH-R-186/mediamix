Anirudh Ravichander ,is born on October 16, 1990, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the son of actor Ravi Raghavendra and the nephew of superstar Rajinikanth. Anirudh showed an early interest in music and began learning piano at a young age. He later pursued formal music education and training in Western classical music. Anirudh made his debut as a composer with the film 3 (2012), directed by Aishwarya R. Dhanush. The song "Why This Kolaveri Di" from the film became a viral sensation on YouTube and across social media. After 3, Anirudh composed music for a series of successful films, including Ethir Neechal (2013), Velaiilla Pattadhari (2014), Kaththi (2014), Vedalam (2015), Remo (2016), Petta (2019), Master (2021), and Jailer (2023).
